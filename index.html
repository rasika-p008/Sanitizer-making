🧴

What is Hand Sanitizer?

Hand sanitizer is an antiseptic liquid or gel used to reduce infectious agents on the hands. It typically contains 60–95% alcohol (ethanol or isopropanol) as the active ingredient, which denatures proteins in bacteria and viruses, effectively killing most pathogens within seconds of application.

🦠

Importance of Hygiene

Proper hand hygiene is one of the most effective ways to prevent the spread of infectious diseases. The World Health Organization estimates that handwashing alone can reduce diarrheal disease risk by 40% and respiratory infections by up to 21%. Sanitizers serve as a quick alternative when soap and water are unavailable.

🎯

Objective of the Project

This project aims to understand the chemical formulation of hand sanitizers, explore the role of each ingredient, create a laboratory-grade sanitizer using WHO-recommended guidelines, and evaluate its effectiveness. We also aim to spread awareness about the importance of hand hygiene in daily life.

03 · Step-by-Step Process

How We Made It

A systematic laboratory procedure following WHO formulation guidelines.

01

Gather & Sanitize Equipment

Collect all necessary lab equipment: measuring cylinders, glass beakers, stirring rods, and dispensing bottles. Clean all utensils with 70% alcohol and allow to dry completely to ensure a sterile working environment.

02

Measure the Alcohol

Accurately measure 750 ml of Isopropyl Alcohol (99%) using a calibrated measuring cylinder. Pour it into a large, clean glass beaker. Alcohol forms the backbone of the sanitizer and must be measured precisely for efficacy.

03

Add Hydrogen Peroxide

Add 41.7 ml of 3% Hydrogen Peroxide to the alcohol. This ingredient kills any residual bacterial spores that may be present in the base solution, ensuring the final product is free from contamination.

04

Incorporate Glycerin & Aloe Vera

Add 14.5 ml of 98% Glycerin and 100 ml of Aloe Vera Gel. Stir gently using a glass rod. These ingredients act as emollients, preventing skin dryness and irritation from repeated alcohol exposure.

05

Top Up with Distilled Water

Add sterile distilled water to bring the total volume to 1000 ml. Distilled water ensures no additional contaminants are introduced. Mix gently to achieve a uniform solution throughout the mixture.

06

Add Thickener & Fragrance

Dissolve Carbopol 940 separately in a small amount of water and add to the mixture. Neutralize with Triethanolamine drops while stirring until the gel forms. Add 2–3 drops of your preferred essential oil for fragrance.

07

Quality Check & Packaging

Test the pH of the final product (should be 6.0–7.0). Check viscosity and consistency. Allow the mixture to rest for 72 hours before packaging. Fill into clean, labeled dispensing bottles and seal properly.

06 · Conclusion

What We Learned

Reflecting on the outcomes and the broader importance of sanitization.

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Understood the chemical basis of antimicrobial action in alcohol-based sanitizers
  • Learned precise volumetric measurement and laboratory safety protocols
  • Gained hands-on experience with WHO formulation guidelines for sanitizer production
  • Explored the role of each ingredient in efficacy and user experience
  • Practiced quality testing including pH measurement and viscosity assessment
  • Developed teamwork, scientific documentation, and presentation skills

🌍 Importance in Daily Life

  • Hand hygiene is a frontline defense against communicable diseases like COVID-19, flu, and cholera
  • Alcohol-based sanitizers kill 99.9% of pathogens within 30 seconds of contact
  • Affordable homemade sanitizers can reach communities with limited access to commercial products
  • Understanding formulations empowers people to make informed hygiene decisions
  • Proper sanitization reduces hospital-acquired infections significantly
  • This project bridges classroom chemistry with real-world public health applications

Clean hands save lives. Through this project, we proved that science is not confined to textbooks — it is a tool for building a safer, healthier world for everyone.
